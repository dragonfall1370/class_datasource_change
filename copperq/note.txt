All emails that may have been recorded on Intelligence will be contained within the CanContact or CliContact tables 
(The CanID and CliID fields will relate these records to the Candidate and Client Tables respectively)

------------------
All documents are stored in BLOB fields in the following tables, again CanID and CliID fields will relate these to Candidates and Client tables

COMPANY:
CliFiles – Files for Clients

CANDIDATE:
CanFiles – Files for Candidates
CVblob – Original CVs usually Word Documents
fCVblob – the formatted CV for the Candidate
PhotoBlob – The photo of the candidate
